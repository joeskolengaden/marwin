About Us

The MARWIN rover is designed to be modular and customizable according to various explorational needs. The basic use of MARWIN will be to avail rapid deployable rover solutions in human exploration of other planets. The working of space rover cannot be exhibited to the public directly and it is because of high maintenance costs. There is a lack of open-source projects that support space exploration and related technology. There is no available prototype or even design for rapid deployable modular rovers.
