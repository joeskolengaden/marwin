Problem Statement

To develop an open source space rover by utilizing all possible open source materials & resources and also make the rover ﬂexible deployable for multiple purposes including research, rescue, military and defence purposes.

Prior detection systems repurpose classiﬁers or localizers to perform detection. They apply the model to an image at multiple locations and scales. High scoring regions of the image are considered detections.

Design

The MARWIN rover is designed to be modular and customizable according to various explorational needs. The basic use of MARWIN will be to avail rapid deployable rover solutions in human exploration of other planets.

Motivation

Ever growing demand and developments in space exploration industry have inspired us to create MARWIN. Rapid industrialization and entry of private ﬁrms in space industry have opened up new possibilities for startups.
